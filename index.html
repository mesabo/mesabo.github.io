About me

Franck Junior Aboya MESSOU

PhD candidate · Applied Informatics

Graduate School of Science and Engineering · YuLab
Hosei University · IIST Koganei Campus, Tokyo

Researcher AI / Backend Engineer Full-stack Engineer

I am a doctoral researcher at the YuLab, Graduate School of Science and Engineering (Applied Informatics), Hosei University, IIST Koganei Campus. My work develops AI, small large language model (sLM LLM), and IoT methods for the stability and resilience of smart grids and IoT systems, with a focus on time-series forecasting, spectral reprogramming, and graph-based control. All experiments run on dedicated multi-GPU servers I provision and maintain.

Outside research, I build production AI / backend systems and full-stack web and mobile applications, and work with partners across Japan, the Gulf, and West Africa.

Research focus

  • AI sLM LLM for power-system stability and grid resilience

  • Spectral reprogramming of frozen small language models (sLM) and LLMs for time-series tasks

  • IoT and edge intelligence for distributed energy resources

  • Co-optimization of energy dispatch and communication routing

  • Graph neural networks over electrical-grid topologies

  • Federated learning, knowledge distillation across IoT-edge devices

Engineering focus

  • Production ML pipelines (training, inference, deployment) on dedicated GPU servers
  • IoT data ingestion and edge model deployment
  • Backend services in Python ( Flask, FastAPI) and Node.js, with REST and GraphQL
  • Data layers: MongoDB · PostgreSQL · MySQL
  • Frontend ( React) and mobile ( Flutter / Dart)
  • Cloud: Google Cloud · containers with Docker · CI with GitHub Actions
